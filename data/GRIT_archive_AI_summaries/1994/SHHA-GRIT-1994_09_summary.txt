The SHHA President has reaffirmed the enforceability of covenants, hence their prioritization in the SHHA agenda. A new SHHA attorney has been appointed specifically to support covenant enforcement. A dedicated committee is in place for identifying and legally addressing covenant violations. Despite mitigative efforts, false burglar alarms remain problematic. The Membership Drive has been fruitful, adding 130 new members to the association. The Architectural Review Committee and the Covenants are together looking into the tramway light glare issue. A decision regarding the County Line intersection traffic lights is still pending. Recycling efforts are still underway for Sandia Heights. The proposed hang gliding site at Pino has been discarded following residents' concerns.

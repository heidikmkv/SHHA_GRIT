The Sandia Homeowners Association (SHHA) is lead by Jim Stewart (President), Eric Faulring (Vice President), Martin Kirk (Secretary), Randy Tripp (Treasurer), and a Board comprised of eight members who also chair several committees. The SHHA offers open communication and invites homeowners to get involved. It further provides various services such as notary services and free copies, and conducts meetings frequently. The latest Board meeting is scheduled for April 10, 2024. Key issues of interest include a detailed guide on household radon mitigation, featuring active and passive systems, safety guidelines, and strategies. Health risk presented by radon and its mitigation are critically emphasized. The association supports local artists, with notable recent features on artists Barb and Lars, boasting unique and evolving styles. PNM, an approved company by ACC, gives advice on the installation of rooftop photovoltaic (PV) solar systems, warning about common pitfalls. SHHA recommends wildfire precautions, advising residents to maintain a clean, debris free environment in and around their properties. The Association welcomes inquiries and new members year round, with Rene Kessel as the point of contact. Lastly, SHHA is located at 12700 San Rafael Ave. NE Ste. 3, Albuquerque, NM 87122 and operates from Monday Thursday, 9 AM to 4 PM, and Friday, 9 AM to 12 PM. The office remains open during holidays.
